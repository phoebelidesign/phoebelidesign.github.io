Phoebe is a Melbourne-based visual designer, interested in exploring the harmony between function and abstraction. She blends a structured design approach rooted in geometry, with a more experimental style. Her work often incorporates tactile methods such as printmaking, analogue photography and collage.


She studied Graphic Design and User Experience Design at the University of Melbourne. She is passionate about music, cinema, literature and a million other things because she develops a fascination for most things she comes across.
