Phoebe is a Naarm/Melbourne-based visual designer, originally from Aotearoa (New Zealand). Interested in materiality and texture, she likes to incorporate tactile processes such as printmaking, analogue photography and collage into her process.

She is also passionate about music, cinema, literature, and a million other things because she develops a fascination for most things she comes across.
