Counterpart

(Publication)

An experimental newspaper reflecting on my lived experience of twinhood.

An investigation into the idea of existence as a constituent of a whole, between symmetry and duality, as well as the commodification of twin identity in the media. A newspaper format is used to nod toward the accessibility and misinterpretation of twinhood.

Includes original photography and text; printed on 45gsm newsprint.
