Phoebe is a New Zealand-born Chinese woman. She is a Naarm/Melbourne-based visual designer specialising in art direction, typography & publication, research and identity.

Her practice often explores documentation and the personal archive. She is also fascinated with materiality and texture, always interested in incorporating tactile methods into her process.

Website designed and developed by Phoebe. Resume available upon request.
