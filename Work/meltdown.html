Meltdown

Brand identity

Meltdown festival is not ‘just about music … it’s a feast for all your senses, a gathering of hearts and souls.’

Meltdown promises an immersive once-in-a-lifetime experience of experimentation, innovation, diversity, inclusion, community engagement and cultural exchange.

The direction is stylised, arresting, cinematic and bold, reflecting how highly anticipated and iconic the event is; along with an air of experimentation as a nod towards the festival’s values. A mix of dynamic sophistication and gritty innovation.
