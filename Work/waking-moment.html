Waking Moment (2023)

Short film

An experimental short film exploring fleeting moments of stillness - decisive moments of clarity that vanish almost immediately. Like objects left in darkness picking up a faint sheen of reflected light from time to time.

Inspired by excerpt: “I need only pause before it and I forget the passage of time.” (In Praise of Shadows - Jun'ichirō Tanizaki,1933)

Frames include both digital video and 35mm film, collaged and scanned. Sound includes found audio and edited instrumental from Sour Times by Portishead.
