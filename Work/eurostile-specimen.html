Eurostile Specimen

Type specimen

A type specimen for Eurostile, a 1962 typeface by Aldo Novarese.

Novarese describes Eurostile as a 'synthetic expression of our times'. It was designed to be a symbol of its era's civilisation, capturing the zeitgeist of the 60s. Similar to Novarese's inspiration for the typeface stemming from the era's mid century architecture, my type specimen is inspired by architectural blueprints.
