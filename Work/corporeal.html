Corporeal

Photobook

An exploration of demeanour and disposition through physical form.

Investigating ephemerality, moments of tenderness. What is revealed through gesture in the absence of facial expression?

Crafted using 90gsm vellum and ink, leveraging transparency to highlight how all captured moments together form a comprehensive study, parts of a greater whole; multiple frames are visible simultaneously as all frames are interdependent.
