Corporeal

90gsm vellum, ink

A photobook exploring demeanour and disposition through corporeal form.

Investigating ephemerality, moments of tenderness. What is revealed through gesture in the absence of facial expression?

Leveraging transparency to highlight how all captured moments together form a comprehensive study, constituents of a whole; multiple frames are visible simultaneously as all frames are interdependent.
